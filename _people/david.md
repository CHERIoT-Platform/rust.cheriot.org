Chisnall joined the CHERI project in 2012 to lead the languages and compiler strand of the research programme.
He moved to Microsoft in 2018 where, among other things, he create and led the CHERIoT project.
He is responsible for the original CHERI LLVM implementation and several key aspects of the CHERI and CHERIoT ISAs.
He has been an LLVM committer since 2008.
